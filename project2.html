Project Overview

Hanssites News Blog is a personal platform created to share the latest news, in-depth analysis, and personal insights on a variety of topics. The blog aims to be a source of reliable information and thoughtful commentary for its readers.

Developed on a popular blogging platform, the focus was on creating a clean, readable, and easily navigable space where content is the main star. The design prioritizes user experience, ensuring that readers can comfortably engage with the articles on any device.

Key Features

Topical Articles

Features well-researched articles on current events, technology, and finance.

Personal Commentary

Offers unique perspectives and analysis, going beyond just reporting the news.

Reader Engagement

Includes a comments section on each post to foster discussion and community interaction.

Content Categorization

Uses tags and categories to organize posts, making it easy for readers to find topics of interest.
